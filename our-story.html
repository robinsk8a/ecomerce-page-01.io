Our Story

Founded in the Northeast Ohio area in 1962, Home Appliance began like many other small, local companies-with hard work built on traditional values like honesty, steadfast service, and integrity. Over the years, they've had their ups and downs: moving to new showrooms, enduring recessions, and managing a huge flood over 10 years ago. Through it all, Home Appliance held onto a vision to be the best at what they do.

From showrooms filled to the brim with every appliance imaginable to creating the largest delivery, installation, service, and repair fleets in Ohio, Home Appliance has kept its focus on one thing-the customer.

Today, owners Brian and Beth Grayson lead the Home Appliance team with a passion for creating a first-class shopping experience for thousands of customers each year. Discover what makes Home Appliance a destination for shoppers across Cleveland and Northeast Ohio. Visit any of our three showrooms and enjoy an appliance shopping experience thoughtfully designed just for you.

Brian Grayson offers this simple message to customers new and old: "Beth and I can tell you many stories of selling, delivering, and servicing appliances late into the night for customers. But the greatest benefit of owning Home Appliance is the relationships we have developed with our customers. We're grateful for the success we and our team have experienced, but we cherish our friendships most of all. Maybe we'll see you around one of our showrooms sometime. We never stop making new friends!"
